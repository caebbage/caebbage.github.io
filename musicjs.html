Music.js

About.

Once upon a time, a certain cae wanted to make a script for forums to insert a music player into posts. The problem was, the music player code they found was bad at its job, so they made a new script from scratch. This is that script.

Features.



  • Uses jQuery, and does not use Flash to operate.
  • Styling and structure of player itself is all modifiable.
  • Progress timestamps, and a progress bar that can be repositioned via dragging or clicking.
