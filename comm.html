Rules.

aka the terms of service.

  • I have a right to decline your commission. It usually comes down to concerns I won't be able to draw the subject well.
    • Won't do: mechas, anthros (kemonomimi ok), nsfw, real people
  • Payment is exclusively through Paypal in USD. An invoice will be sent to your account once the commission is accepted. There may be a bit added to cover paypal's fees.
  • Once paid, the commission cannot be cancelled unless by me. This is to discourage cancelling the commission while I'm in the middle of working on it. I will refund in the case of cancellations.
  • Please be patient! I have other things to do than just drawing and on some days I simply won't be able to complete a commission well.
    • If you are concerned about commission progress, please do not bother me every single day about it. Constant nagging makes me want to complete it less, and I may cancel altogether.
  • Commissioned pieces are non-commercial. The art itself still belongs to me, and I may use it for other purposes later on (hint: more samples). If you actually do want to use it commercially, speak to me on discord.
