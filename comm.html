Terms of Service

  • I have the right to decline any commission offer if I believe I cannot suitably deliver the product, am uncomfortable, etc.
  • Non-commercial use only! (Though if you're serious about that message me and we can probably talk it out.)
  • Payment is strictly through paypal, in USD. I will send you an invoice to pay through.
  • Work on the commission begins after payment. I will provide at minimum a sketch during the process, as well as the final image once the sketch has been reviewed and approved.
  • Once paid for, the commission is non-refundable unless I, the artist, request it. (This is to prevent work from being wasted.)
  • Once the sketch has been approved, no requests for major adjustments will be considered unless it is a technical error eg. getting a design aspect incorrect.
    • Minor adjustments are still acceptable, eg. adjusting the color, fixing minor details, etc. If in doubt, ask anyways, it'll usually be acceptable.
  • Asking for status updates is fine, but please don't repeatedly pester about when it will be finished. It usually will be complete within a week or so anyways but I won't feel bad postponing if I'm annoyed.
  • I absolutely will not draw: NSFW, very muscular characters, MLP
    • Anything else is a probable yes or can be negotiated. (I am human though and can't remember everything, so pls don't hit me if I decide I'm not comfortable w something)
    • Please remember that my style is geared towards humanoid characters.

Options

All varieties are fully colored. Experimental options may not be consistent in style/method to previous pieces.
Prices are base prices, in USD, and there may be added cost depending on the complexity of what you want. (This will be discussed before payment if that is the case.) There will also be an additional amount to cover paypal fees (typically 1~2 dollars).
  • headshot: $30
  • Half-body: $60
  • Fullbody: $80
  • EXPERIMENTAL - chibis: $40
Simple backgrounds consisting of basic shapes and/or simple/premade patterns can be requested without extra cost. As with everything else, other additions can be negotiated.
